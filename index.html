About Kahũa

"Kahũa" is a premium coffee brand that hails from the lush green hills of Thika, Kenya. Known for its exceptional coffee beans, grown on rich volcanic soil, and bathed in plenty of sunshine.

Kahũa prides itself on using only the best beans and traditional roasting methods to produce a bold and flavorful cup of coffee. Savor the smooth taste and rich aroma with every sip and treat yourself to a true testament of coffee-making heritage

How to prepare

Measure a tablespoon of coffee for each cup of coffee

Wet the coffee with little water, pour the rest of the remaining water over a 30 - second duration

Sieve & enjoy your great cup of Kahũa coffee.
